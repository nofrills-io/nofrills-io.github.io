Why us

Our expertise

An old German saying: One butt can not ride two horses. That's why we focus on what we do best: Full Stack Mobile Development. Specifically, we offer mobile app development for IOS & Android devices, accompanied by backend development & DevOps. Our core competence is the development of apps for Android and iOS - supported by a robust and scalable backend API architecture.

Full-cycle native iOS & Android and back-end application development services, from the initial architectural & technical planning of your project, until its final deployment, that's our job.

Our developers are experienced with advanced features like iBeacons, IoT, Augmented / Virtual Reality, Location Services, Artificial Intelligence, Service Architecture, API’s, Containers, Kubernetes, Azure and platform scalability.

Our principles and standards

We practice no-nonsense software development, using well known coding practices such as test driven (TDD) and behavior driven development (BDD).

We're sharp, efficient, and pay attention to details. It's amazing what a difference this can make.

With our no-frills promise, we aim for development with zero waste, and we keep things simple, using all the resources to the optimal. Whenever we can, we avoid process overhead, so that our developers can focus on their expertise: creating the best solutions by functional code.

Our consultancy is NB, non-sales, advice. We help you to understand your situation and together we will find and examine alternatives according to sense and economy, taking into account the individual logic of your project.

We forgo the bloat and bureaucracy that plagues our industry. We don't have 5 layers of management. When you talk to us, you talk to us – not someone representing us - a small team of experts.
